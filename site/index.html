The Context Pack workflow

Full-project understanding. Focused instructions.

Cenro does not paste your whole repository—or a vague summary—into a model. It maps the project locally, improves the intent, then selects the smallest useful set of files with a visible reason for each one.

A boundary you can inspect

A cloud worker does not get your whole drive.

The repository map, secret filters, and relevance work happen locally. Before anything leaves your device, Cenro spells out the provider, model, selected paths, character count, and reason each item earned its place.
